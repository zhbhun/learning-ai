FAQ

Questions, answered

How long does it take to set up Nimbus?

Most teams are seeing live data within 5 minutes. Drop in our JS snippet or install an SDK, and autocapture starts collecting page views and clicks immediately.

Do you offer a free plan?

Yes — the Starter plan is free forever, with 10k events per month and core dashboards. No credit card required to sign up.

How is Nimbus different from Google Analytics?

Nimbus is built for product teams, not marketing attribution. You get user-level journeys, funnels, cohorts, and retention — with real-time data and no sampling.

Is my data private and compliant?

Nimbus is SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR and CCPA compliant, and offers EU data residency on Growth and Enterprise plans. We never sell or share your data.

Can I migrate historical data from another tool?

Yes. Our importers support Amplitude, Mixpanel, and CSV/warehouse backfills, so your retention charts keep their full history.
